ACCORDION POTATOES

A spin on the standard Fired potatoes.

Accordion potatoes are perfect if you want to elevate your normal potato side dish! With such an eye-catching shape,
they are perfect for an impressive dish to wow your guest!
And get this, they are SUPER easy to make!

The recipe below is designed to act as a guide, and not something to be followed prescriptively. Play with your food! Adjust amounts based on your tastes.

Ingredients

Serves as many people as you want!

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic Butter Mixture
  • Skewers

How To Make Accordion Potatoes?

  • Step 1: Peel the potatoes. Then, cut the ends of the potatoes.
    Cut the potato lengthwise into 1/4 to 3/8 inch slices. Do not cut larger than 1/2 inch.
    Larger slices are slightly harder to manipulate and also do not get as crispy when baked.

  • Step 2: Place the potato horizontally. Place the skewer on both sides of the potato.
    Use skewers as a base so that you do not cut all the way through the potato and cut vertical lines.
    Keep the lines close to each other so you make the accordion shape.
    (For those that like to get technical, try to keep the lines no more than 1/16 inches apart.)

  • Step 3: In the same fashion, flip the potato over and cut diagonal lines.

  • Step 4: Push the skewer through the potato.
    (I find it best to leave the potato on the cutting board,
    place my hand over the potato to hold it down, and gently push the skewer through the center of the potato.
    Please be careful so that you do not pierce yourself with the skewer.)
    Gently stretch out the potato, starting with the bottom and work towards the middle.

  • Step 5 : While you are slicing the potatoes, keep the potatoes that you have already cut in cold water.
    This helps to keep the potatoes from turning brown.
    This also helps to break down the excess starch which will help make delicious crispy potatoes when they are baked.

  • Step 6: Now, brush the garlic-herb butter onto both sides of the potatoes.

  • Step 7: Now Fry them or Air Fry them at 375 degree Farenheit and Server them with Ketchup.
